REAN HealthGuru mobile platform developed by REAN Foundation helps users set their own health and wellness goals that suit their priorities and lifestyle and help them achieve those goals. This is achieved via our 6 pillars of self-care including nutrition, exercise, medication management, stress management, vitals tracking and identifying disease symptoms. Healthkit and Google Fit data integration with the other datapoints being collected by our app will help users track, review and take actions on various interconnected health parameters.
